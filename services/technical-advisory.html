Technical Consultancy & Advisory

Our technical consultancy and advisory services provide expert, independent guidance to support informed decision-making across technology, security and compliance. We work closely with leadership and technical teams to assess current capability, identify risks and opportunities, and deliver practical recommendations aligned with business objectives and regulatory requirements. Whether providing strategic oversight or hands-on advisory support, we help organisations navigate complexity, strengthen technical foundations and implement solutions that are effective, proportionate and sustainable.

Examples

  • Technical strategy & roadmap development
  • Systems architecture review & optimisation
  • Technology audits & gap analysis
  • Risk, resilience & continuity planning
  • Independent technical second opinions
  • Vendor & supplier technical evaluation
  • Technical due diligence (pre-investment / pre-procurement)
  • Technical governance, change & delivery assurance

Cyber Security & Information Assurance

Our cyber security and information assurance services help organisations protect sensitive information, manage cyber risk and demonstrate a robust security posture to clients, regulators and auditors. We work across people, process and technology to assess security controls, identify vulnerabilities and strengthen governance in line with recognised standards and best practice. By aligning technical safeguards with risk management, compliance requirements and operational realities, we support organisations in reducing exposure, maintaining trust and evidencing effective information security management.

Examples

  • Cyber security risk assessments
  • Information security frameworks & controls
  • Data protection & GDPR technical support
  • Security policies, procedures & documentation
  • Incident response planning
  • Security awareness & staff training
  • Third-party & supplier security assessments
  • Vulnerability management & security assurance

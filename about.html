About SM Technical Consultancy

A multi-disciplinary technical and compliance consultancy with a strong hybrid background across technology and compliance.

Our Expertise

Technical & Compliance Consultancy

We are a multi-disciplinary technical and compliance consultancy specialising in ISO standards implementation, gap analysis, information security, risk management, and operational process optimisation. With a strong hybrid background across technology and compliance, we support organisations in building robust, audit-ready management systems that align seamlessly with real-world operations — a capability particularly valued by fast-scaling SaaS and technology-driven businesses.

We provide end-to-end support across ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018 and ISO 27001:2022, including detailed ISO gap analyses to assess maturity, alignment of technical environments with ISO 27001 Annex A controls, full management system design and implementation, risk assessment and treatment planning, internal auditing and audit preparation, and ongoing compliance governance and continuous improvement support.

For SaaS organisations, we also specialise in supplier security questionnaires, customer due-diligence responses and compliance evidence collation. We help teams meet enterprise, NHS and public-sector assurance requirements with clarity and confidence — simplifying technical control explanations, closing compliance gaps and ensuring submissions reflect a strong, credible security posture.

Our technical expertise allows us to translate ISO requirements into practical, implementable solutions — whether advising on access control models, logging and monitoring, vulnerability management, operational workflows, or the documentation needed to demonstrate compliance and drive security and scalability. We apply a structured yet flexible methodology that works effectively in agile environments where speed, scalability and efficiency are critical.

We are well placed to provide strategic technical advice to organisations aspiring to build or enhance upon an existing internal IT Department, through internal management or vendor relationships.

We are known for delivering clear, actionable guidance without unnecessary complexity; enabling organisations to achieve certification, reduce risk, meet client assurance demands and build resilient, scalable management systems.
